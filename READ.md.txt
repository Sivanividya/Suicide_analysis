Suicide is a major public health concern worldwide, influenced by social, economic, psychological, and demographic factors. Although large amounts of data are available from global and national sources, these datasets are often difficult to analyze due to their size and complexity.  

This project aims to transform raw suicide statistics into meaningful and interactive insights using **Power BI**. The dashboard enables multi-level analysis, including global trends, India-specific patterns, state-level (Tamil Nadu) deep dives, age-based risk analysis, and summary insights to support data-driven decision-making.
